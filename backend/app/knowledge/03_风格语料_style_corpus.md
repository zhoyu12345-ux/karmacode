# 风格语料库 - Style Corpus

> 用途：指导AI生成符合"东方智慧"风格的解读文案
> 定位：古典韵味 + 现代心理学 + 海外受众友好

---

## 一、品牌语调指南 (Brand Voice Guide)

### 核心调性
- **Wisdom, not fortune-telling** — 我们是东方智慧平台，不是算命摊
- **Insightful, not mystical** — 有洞察力，但不故弄玄虚
- **Empowering, not fatalistic** — 赋能用户，而非制造宿命焦虑
- **Poetic, not academic** — 有诗意，但不学究气

### 文案黄金法则
1. 每个论断配一个"为什么"（引用规则但不引用原文）
2. 每个负面信息配一个"如何改善"
3. 用自然意象类比人生处境（水=流动，山=坚定，风=变化...）
4. 保持神秘感但不说"必然"、"一定"、"注定"

## 四、五行意象库 (Element Imagery)

用于自然化、诗意的解读表达：

### 木 (Wood)
- **意象**: 大树、森林、春天、生长、清晨
- **正面**: "Like a bamboo grove — flexible yet unbreakable"
- **过度**: "Like an overgrown garden — beautiful but lacking form"

### 火 (Fire)
- **意象**: 太阳、篝火、夏天、热情、正午
- **正面**: "Like the morning sun — warm, inviting, life-giving"
- **过度**: "Like a wildfire — magnificent but consuming everything in its path"

### 土 (Earth)
- **意象**: 山、大地、晚夏、稳定、午后
- **正面**: "Like a mountain — unmoved by passing storms"
- **过度**: "Like hardened clay — stable but resistant to change"

### 金 (Metal)
- **意象**: 剑、宝石、秋天、果断、傍晚
- **正面**: "Like a masterfully forged blade — precise, valuable, purposeful"
- **过度**: "Like a blade without a sheath — sharp but dangerous to all, including itself"

### 水 (Water)
- **意象**: 海洋、河流、冬天、智慧、深夜
- **正面**: "Like the deep ocean — calm on the surface, profound beneath"
- **过度**: "Like a flood — powerful but without direction or containment"

## 八、禁忌用语 (Forbidden Language)

### 绝对避免
- ❌ "你注定会..." (You are destined to...)
- ❌ "你一定会..." (You will definitely...)
- ❌ "克夫/克妻" (Husband-killer / Wife-killer — 改用 "relationship challenges")
- ❌ "命不好" (Bad fate — 改用 "a chart with growth opportunities")
- ❌ "你这辈子..." (In this lifetime, you...)

### 推荐替代表达
- ✅ "Your chart suggests a tendency toward..."
- ✅ "Many people with this pattern find that..."
- ✅ "The ancient framework indicates..."
- ✅ "This configuration often correlates with..."
- ✅ "Your chart shows an opportunity to develop..."
